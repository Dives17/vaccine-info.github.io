take precautions before Vaccinated

things you should DO

Eat heavy meal

Eat heavy meal before getting vaccinated.Foods rich in fibre are crucial for a relaxed body and a powered up immune system.

always wear a mask

Wearing a mask reduces the spread of infectious droplets, especially when someone speaks, coughs or sneezes, for diseases like COVID-19

drink water

Staying hydrated is key to good health, especially when you are getting your COVID vaccination.

things you should NOT DO

avoid close contact with animals

Some coronaviruses that infect animals can be spread to people and then spread between people, but this is rare

avoid close contact with peoples

Avoid close contact with people who are sick. If possible, maintain 6 feet between the person who is sick and other household members.

avoid crowded area

Being in crowds like in restaurants, bars, fitness centers, or movie theaters puts you at higher risk for COVID-19
